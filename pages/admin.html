Writing Tips

Headline: Make it specific and outcome-focused. "How to 3× Your Meta Ads ROAS" beats "Tips for Ads".
Structure: Use H2 headings every 200–300 words. Break up text with bullets and quotes.
Length: Aim for 800–1,500 words for most posts. Use the word counter above.
HTML Tags: Content is rendered as HTML. Use <h2>, <p>, <ul>, <li>, <strong>, <em>, <blockquote>.
